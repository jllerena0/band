Favorite Albums

Hymns in Dissonance
A heavy album that goes back to their earlier roots, but with a much darker undertone. It's brutal, fast, and heavy. Every song is a banger and you'll hear the lowest vocals a human can do.
This is Exile
An amazing sophomore album that was a staple of the MySpace deathcore scene at the time. This album showcases their technical skills, grooves, and guttural vocals.
Somatic Defilement
Their debut album which most longtime fans love. Its raw production adds to the nostalgia of the 2000's with its dark lyrics that tell a gruesome horror story. It's complex and all over the place.
